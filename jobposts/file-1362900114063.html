Job Description
Amazon Mechanical Turk is a marketplace for work. Business use Mechanical Turk to crowdsource tasks to a global community of over 500K Workers. We are constantly trying to make more informed decisions using advanced data mining techniques. We are looking for hands-on senior researcher to help us grow and drive data-based innovations. This position is a uniquely demanding position requiring creativity, the ability to deal with ambiguity and execution/delivery skills.
The data scientist is responsible for solving complex big-data problems in crowdsourcing using data mining, machine learning, statistical analysis and computational economics. An ideal candidate should strong depth and breadth knowledge in machine learning, data mining and statistics. The candidate should have reasonable programming and design skills to manipulate unstructured data, build prototypes that work on massive datasets and work with an engineering team to deploy these algorithms into production systems. The candidate should be able to apply business knowledge to do broad data analysis as a precursor to modeling and to provide valuable business intelligence.
Basic Qualifications
  • MS in Statistics, Computer Science, or Mathematics
  • Experience in data mining, machine learning techniques and statistics
  • The ability to distill problem definitions, models, constraints from informal business requirements; and to deal with ambiguity and competing objectives.
  • Ability to translate business requirements into solutions
  • Ability to analyze mined data and extrapolate conclusions
Preferred Qualifications
  • PhD preferred, but not required
  • Superior verbal and written communication skills, ability to convey rigorous mathematical concepts and considerations to non-experts.
  • Solid written and oral communication skills for presenting data findings across the company
  • Domain knowledge and experience with the following areas:
  • Statistics, machine learning, data mining, natural language processing , optimization, Text Mining
  • Experience with large data sets, experiment design and analysis
  • Experience with crowdsourcing
  • Experience with economic or marketplace model
  • Experience with forecasting and prediction, social science datasets or campaign analysis preferred
  • Strong knowledge of SQL
  • Strong programming skills and computer science fundamentals
