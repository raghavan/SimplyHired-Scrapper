Data Anly Scientist Lvl 3 PCCI Clinical Innovations Center

Dallas, TX

PRIMARY PURPOSE

Responsible for all phases of data analytics (extraction, analysis, manipulation, synthesis, summary, validation, testing, and maintenance) of analytics tools, statistical models, and algorithms for clinical and operational initiatives identified by the Parkland Center for Clinical Innovation (PCCI).

MINIMUM SPECIFICATIONS

Education:
-Must have a Master's degree in Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Informatics, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
-Prefer Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Informatics, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
-Prefer Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree.

Experience:
-Must have seven years of research or applied experience in designing and building healthcare software and/or technology in a scientific environment.
-Must have experience working with a combination of Bayesian analysis and decision making, nonparametric Bayes, machine learning, artificial intelligence, pattern recognition, and graphical probability models.
-Must have experience writing, reviewing, editing, submitting, completing and obtaining peer-reviewed manuscripts and grants.
-Prefer experience working with electronic medical record (EMR) systems and data.
-Prefer experience completing and obtaining U.S. patents.
-Prefer experience in solution development for healthcare delivery systems.

Certification/Registration/Licensure:
-None.

Skills or Special Abilities:
-Must demonstrate outstanding and advanced statistical knowledge, skills and experience with the emphasis on Bayesian analysis and decision making, nonparametric Bayes, machine learning, artificial intelligence, pattern recognition, and graphical probability models. Must also be experienced and skillful at traditional statistical methods and modeling including various types of logistic regressions, missing data imputation, and model development.
-Must be familiar with the current state of advanced analysis for big data and the trends in other related fields or disciplines.
-Must have excellent statistical analysis knowledge, skills and abilities in advanced data management and statistical programming using software such as STATA, SPSS, SAS, or R.
-Knowledge of EPIC/Clarity database and other Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems and understands the architecture of data structure.
-Able to architect systems and algorithms in various healthcare related domains, including structured and non-structured EMR (electronic medical record) fields, different sources, and the integration efforts.
-Must be able to demonstrate the ability to take initiatives in evaluating projects by identifying key partners and collaborators, and proposing strategies and action plans.
-Must have a clear understanding of the challenges and complexities of the projects and be able to formulate and propose strategies and action plans.
-Strong capability to build models and utilize statistical methods including data imputation, time series, logistic regression, survival analysis, neural networks and other modeling techniques.
-Expertise to perform exploratory data analysis and implement propensity models.
-Create performance metrics and tracking processes to measure the effectiveness of the predictive modeling efforts.
-Coordinate access and perform data transformation to support model development and validation.
-Utilize data mining techniques and information retrieval methods to gather business intelligence.
-Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills. Must be able to adjust communications to fit the intended audience and message.
-Must have excellent presentation skills, with the ability to present in smooth, efficient, and easy-to-understand fashion. Able to engage audience during presentations to achieve intended goals and effects.
-Must have excellent problem solving skills to include the ability to design novel algorithms for problem solving, which may include data cleaning, data mining, feature selection, data clustering, information visualization, and other pattern recognition methodologies that embrace problems from project design and process, data related issues, database development obstacles, etc.
-Must have excellent customer service skills. Must be attentive to new requests and able to direct the requests to proper source or staff for follow up, ensuring customers are always updated on the status/progress of their request, and remain committed to the customers until project closure.
-Must have excellent organizational skills. Must be effective and efficient at marshaling multiple resources to get things done. Must be able to work on multiple tasks at once without losing track. Must be able to foresee and improvise.
-Must be familiar with common software systems, including operating systems, office systems, software development environments, database management systems and result reporting systems. Must be familiar with existing information retrieval techniques and natural language processing methods.
-Must be familiar with project management tools or methodologies to ensure timely delivery of end results, with the ability to move projects forward when projects are stranded by various reasons. Ensures the overall quality and accuracy of projects including sub-components.
-Must be familiar with integrated data analysis methods, workflow, and user interface.
