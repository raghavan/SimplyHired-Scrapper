Senior Data Scientist

Job Location: San Bruno, CA

Shift
0

Position Summary
The Senior Data Scientist will be responsible for designing analytical models/tools to create an innovative supply chain network of the future. The Network Strategy & Optimization team develops and executes strategies to optimally allocate a large assortment of product to deliver the best customer experience. This person will be responsible for bringing analytical rigor and statistics to challenge existing processes and identify opportunities by working with large amounts of historical data to optimize for the future. This person will work with cross-functional business partners within Operations along with other business areas like Merchandise Planning, Product Management, IT and Engineering to support various initiatives. This position offers opportunity for immediate impact to the business through the development of effective models and metrics.

Position Description
ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:
(1) Supply Chain and Logistics Optimization (90%)
• Generate complex analysis, presentations, and recommendations in support of the team to improve speed to customer and reduce the operating costs within business constraints
• Dive into large data sets to analyze root causes and implement long term solutions in systems or processes to optimize the supply chain
• Identify opportunities in existing processes and create new methods to attack the problem leveraging statistical data modeling and optimization tools
• Create optimized predictive modeling techniques in multiple aspects of the forecasting process
• Create models to analyze the network need to support future sales growth with scenario modeling capabilities to understand the sensitivity to multiple variables
• Model to optimize for the balance between minimal cost and maximize customer experience
• Maintain and improve Llamasoft model to provide recommendations for future supply chain needs
• Identify and evaluate opportunities to reduce variable costs by improving the fulfillment network topology, inventory placement, transportation operations and scheduling, fulfillment center processes, and the execution to operational plans.
• Improve the efficiency of capital investment by potentially helping the fulfillment centers to improve storage utilization and the effective use of automation. Finally, you will help create the metrics to quantify improvements to the fulfillment costs (e.g., transportation and labor costs) resulting from the application of these optimization models and tools

(2) Change Management (10%)
• Identifying areas of opportunity and drive results partnering with cross functional partners
• Develop relationships with Wal-Mart functional areas in San Bruno, CA, as well as in Bentonville, AR, to provide visibility to the business and to gather support for key initiatives
• Obtain alignment from relevant business partners on proposed changes and lead discussions to drive change
• Communicate clearly via email, verbally, and presentations. Tailor communications to the appropriate level audience and modify approach as needed for maximum impact.

Minimum Qualifications
1) Knowledge, Skills, Abilities:
Functional Competencies:
• Algorithms, optimization, mathematics, and statistics
• Experience with databases and large data sets
• Experience with large database mining tools (e.g., SAS, Hadoop, R, Stata)
• Experience with network modeling tools (e.g. Llamasoft, CAPS).

Interpersonal Competencies:
• Ability to communicate complex concepts in easy-to-understand terminology
• Effective communication skills at multiple levels of the organization, including management and cross-functional groups
• Team player
• Personal initiative to find opportunities and drive results working independently


2) Preferred Educational Level:
• Master’s degree in a relevant field of Operations Research, Computer Science, Math, or Statistics
• PhD preferred

3) Experience:
• 3+ years of experience in supply chain management, logistics, network design

4) Physical Requirements:
• Required to travel to the Wal-Mart Home Office in Bentonville, Ark. and/or distribution centers, quarterly or as needed
